## Common Questions

**How do I restore my data on a new device?**
Use Export (Settings > Export Data) to create a backup, then Import on your new device. Pro users can also enable iCloud sync.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions on your iPhone.

**My habits disappeared. What happened?**
Check if they were archived (Settings > Archived Habits). Archived habits can be restored anytime.

**How do I delete my data?**
All data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all data. You can also delete individual habits from the habit detail screen.
